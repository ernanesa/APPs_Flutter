Privacy Policy - Fasting Tracker

Last updated: January 16, 2026

1. Introduction

The Fasting Tracker app ("we", "our", or "App") is developed by Ernane Rezende (sa.rezende). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1. User-Provided Data

The app stores fasting session data (start/end times, protocols) locally on your device. This data includes:

  • Fasting session timestamps
  • Selected fasting protocols (16:8, 18:6, etc.)
  • Achievement progress
  • Streak counters
  • App preferences (theme, notifications)

2.2. Automatically Collected Data

  • Google AdMob: For displaying ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address, and usage data. See Google's Privacy Policy.

3. Data Storage

All personal data (fasting sessions, preferences) is stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences. We do not collect, store, or transmit this data to external servers. Uninstalling the app removes all stored data.

4. Third-Party Services

4.1. Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may use cookies and collect:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • IP address
  • Usage statistics

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy and AdMob's data usage.

5. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. The only third-party service that may collect data is Google AdMob for advertising purposes.

7. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data (stored locally on your device)
  • Delete your data (by uninstalling the app or clearing app data)
  • Opt-out of personalized ads (via device settings)

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
